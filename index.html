About

Dynamic Manufacturing Systems is a leader in providing only the finest machine parts and fabrication services to our customers. We have the combined experience to not only handle the most complex of machining task, but we can do the job right the first time at a competitive price, saving our customers time and money.

With our state of the art facility, we can handle one andf two piece orders as werll as large production runs. We have developed a system that allows the customer the ability to work directly with our team of professionals by sending CAD data directly to us and programming these files using Surfcam or CAMWorks (computer aided manufacturing) to genertate the tool path. This assures part accuracy and allows your engineers to be a part of the manufacturing process. This method not only saves time but it allows us to take invested time from your engineers and turn it into a cost saving solution for your company. We are all about forming partnerships with our customers. This is just one method of establishing long and trusted relationships by working together for the commoin goal of 'Total Customer Satisfaction'.

If Quality Assurance is a primary concern, be assured that Dynamic Manufacturing Systems has taken the necessary steps to insure quality by investing into the latest technology of Portable Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM). The "Romer Infinite Arm", advanced unit will allow us to inspect parts and generate reports for part conformance and verification. This versatile light weight unit can be placed inaside the machine to inspect parts without having constly teardowns which saves time and money. The arms connects to the portable computer via WiFi which allows us to record and verify the data taken from the part. With extremely high accuracy, this portable CMM has an 8-foot range that allows us to check small parts all the way up to large bulky parts.

This latest technological marvel also has very powerful software for Reverse Engineering called PC-DEMIS. This software allows us to duplicate old obsolete or complex geometries by scanning a surface with a touch probe and send the data to our Solidworks cad software for cad drawings or reverse engineering or to our CAM Software for direct manufacturing of the part. We have helped customers in the Pharmaceutical, Tobacco, Transportation and the Power industry just to name a few with this advanced technology. With this powerful solution, imagine how beneficial we can be to the operation of your business.

We specialize in Precisiopn Machining, MIG and TIG welding, sheet metal fabrications, complex surfacing found in the mold and die industry, shaft and roll manufacturing, grinding and so much more all in one facility. Rest assured with every request for quote or every order, you will get prompt, professional and courteous service that you expect in a world class company. If you are looking for a specialized process, Dynamic Manufacturing Systems has partnered with only the best approved vendors that offer processes like heat treatment, carbide nitrate, nickel boron, powder coating, anodizing, and many more.

Let us show you how our success can be your success
